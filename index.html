About Chicago's Tree Planting

The Department of Streets and Sanitation's Bureau of Forestry plants trees along the public right-of-way throughout Chicago. The benefits of trees are numerous and ever increasingly important in this age of climate change, in achieving a cleaner, greener, sustainable environment. Chicago property owners who want a new tree planted on their parkways may submit a request via CHI 311 for this free city service.
